# Osekai Community Guidelines

Osekai is a small, community-run website. To keep it usable for everyone:

- **Be respectful.** No harassment, hate speech, or targeted abuse in comments or suggestions.
- **No spam.** Don't post repeated, irrelevant, or self-promotional content.
- **Keep beatmap suggestions on-topic.** Suggestions should genuinely relate to the medal in question.
- **No cheating or exploit encouragement.** Don't use Osekai to promote cheats, hacks, or unfair play.

Moderators may remove content or restrict accounts that break these guidelines. If you see something that shouldn't be here, report it or contact a moderator directly.

Questions or concerns? Reach out at archie@untone.uk.
